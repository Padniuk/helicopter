Wars in wars? Rakesh Sharma, retired Indian lieutenant general

Generals like to say that the best teacher of military strategy is war. As cruel and costly as it may be, it dispels illusions and brings one into contact with reality. What have been the tactical and strategic lessons of the Ukraine conflict so far?
