The Armed Forces of Ukraine killed about 60,430 Russian soldiers between February 24 and October 3, including 320 in the past day alone.

The General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine said this in a  post, according to Ukrinform. Ukrainian defenders also destroyed 2,380 (+3) Russian tanks, 4,991 (+16) armored fighting vehicles, 1,405 (+0) artillery systems, 338 (+1) multiple rocket launchers, 176 (+0) air defense systems, 265 (+1) warplanes, 228 (+1) helicopters, 1,026 (+11) operational and tactical-level unmanned aerial vehicles, 246 (+0) cruise missiles, 15 (+0) warships/cutters, 3,811 (+15) trucks and tankers, and 131 (+0) pieces of specialized equipment. The Russian enemy suffered the greatest losses in the past day in the Kramatorsk and Donetsk directions, the General Staff said.
