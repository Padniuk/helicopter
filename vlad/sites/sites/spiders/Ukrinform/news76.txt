Six civilians have been killed and four injured in Russia’s shelling of the Donetsk region over the past day.

The relevant statement was made by Donetsk Regional Military Administration Head Pavlo Kyrylenko on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “On September 28, 2022, Russians killed six civilians in the Donetsk region: two in Toretsk, one in Bakhmut, one in Pivnichne, one in Blahodatne, one in Mykolaivka. Four more people were injured,” Kyrylenko wrote. At the moment, it is impossible to estimate the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha, Kyrylenko added. mk
