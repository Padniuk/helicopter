Russian troops struck a village in Zaporizhzhia region with Smerch MLRS. One person was killed, four more, including a child, were injured.

"One person was killed and four local residents were injured in strikes launched from Smerch rocket launchers with cluster munitions. A one-year-old girl is among the injured," Oleksandr Starukh, Head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration, posted on  . The child received multiple mine blast injuries. She is currently in a serious condition in the hospital. Other victims also received serious injuries: contusion, shrapnel wounds, severed limbs, torn off fingers.
