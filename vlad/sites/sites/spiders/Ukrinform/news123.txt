A total of 6,278 Ukrainian children have been found since the Russian full-scale invasion started.

The press service of the Ombudsman's Office   this on Facebook, Ukrinform reports. "Information from the ‘Children of War’ state portal for finding missing children as of September 30, 2022: 242 - missing, 7,890 - deported, 6,278 - found,” the Ombudsman's Office said.
