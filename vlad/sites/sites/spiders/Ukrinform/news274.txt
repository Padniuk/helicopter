Enemy Shahed-136 kamikaze drones struck Bila Tserkva town of Kyiv region six times last night.

Oleksiy Kuleba, the head of the Kyiv Regional Military Administration,   this on Telegram, Ukrinform reports. "Kyiv region. Last night, the enemy attacked Bila Tserkva with kamikaze drones of Shahed-136 type. A total of six strikes and explosions. One person was injured. The infrastructure was also damaged," he said. Elimination of the consequences of the enemy attack, including fire extinguishing, continues. Relevant services are working at the site. As many as 57 rescuers and 15 units of equipment of the State Emergency Service of Ukraine are involved.
