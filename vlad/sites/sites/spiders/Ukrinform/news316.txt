Russian troops again struck Zaporizhzhia with missiles, the second such attack since Thursday morning.

Oleksandr Starukh, the head of the Zaporizhzhia regional military administration, said this in a statement on  , Ukrinform reports. "Another missile attack by the enemy. Stay in shelters!" he wrote. Earlier reports said that at 05:00 on October 6, the enemy fired S-300 missiles at the city. Two of them hit residential buildings, leaving two people dead and about ten wounded.
