Russian President Putin's call for additional Russian military forces in Ukraine reflects Russia's problems on the battlefield.

U.S. Secretary of State Antony Blinken said this on  , Ukrinform reports. "President Putin's call for additional Russian military forces in Ukraine reflects Russia's struggles on the battlefield and the fact that President Putin is operating from a position of weakness, not strength," the tweet reads. Russia's full-scale military aggression in Ukraine has been ongoing since February 24. Ukraine's defense forces are fiercely resisting the Russian invaders. On September 21, after declaring a partial mobilization in Russia, President Putin again threatened Western countries with the use of nuclear weapons.
