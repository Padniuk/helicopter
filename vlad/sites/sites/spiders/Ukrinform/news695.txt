The Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed seven air targets over the past day, and the aviation launched up to 10 group airstrikes.

On September 22, the anti-aircraft missile units of the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed seven air targets: three UAVs of operational-tactical level and four Iranian-made Shahed-136 kamikaze drones which the invaders label as "Geran-2". "The Air Force aviation launched up to 10 group airstrikes in different directions. Bombers and attack aircraft eliminated several dozen invaders, up to 20 armored combat vehicles, a platoon stronghold, and other targets," the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine posted on  . In addition, the fighter aircraft used AGM-88 HARM anti-radiation missiles against the enemy's air defense systems. Up to 10 enemy air defense positions were hit.
