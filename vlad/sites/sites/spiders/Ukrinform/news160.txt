On Saturday, October 1, the law of the United States of America on lend-lease for Ukraine entered into force.

Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada Ruslan Stefanchuk   this on Twitter, Ukrinform reports. "Today, the U.S. law on lend-lease for Ukraine has entered into force. This means more different and high-quality weapons," Stefanchuk wrote. He emphasized that Ukraine will definitely return all its territories.
