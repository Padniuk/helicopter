Fragments of a Russian Su-34 fighter bomber have been discovered in the recently retaken town of Lyman in the Donetsk region.

The press service of the Ukrainian Air Force said this in a statement on  , Ukrinform reports. "Wreckage of a Russian Su-34 bomber, RF-81852, was found in Lyman. Earlier, it was not posted on the internet so it's another aircraft that can be added to the confirmed losses of the Russians," the report said.
