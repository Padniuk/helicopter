Donetsk Regional Military Administration has shown the military equipment left by Russian troops when retreating.

The relevant video was posted by Donetsk Regional Military Administration on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. The video shows a large number of the Russian military equipment units destroyed, including tanks. “In Bohorodychne, Russians left military equipment when retreating. Some vehicles were destroyed with our artillery, and some are suitable for use. After repairs, this equipment will serve for the Ukrainian military,” the report states.
