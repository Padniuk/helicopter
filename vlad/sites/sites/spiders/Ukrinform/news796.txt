Due to the armed aggression of the Russian Federation, three civilians were killed and 22 more were wounded in Ukraine over the past day, on September 25.

Kyrylo Tymoshenko, the deputy head of the President’s Office, said in a Telegram  , Ukrinform reports. “Consequences of the armed aggression of the Russian Federation. Victims among the civilian population: three people were killed, 22 people were wounded," Tymoshenko wrote. In particular, three people were killed and 11 more were wounded in Donetsk region, six people were wounded in Kharkiv region, four were wounded in Mykolaiv regions, and one person was wounded in Kherson region. As reported by Ukrinform, the troops of the Russian Federation fired 130 MLRS and heavy artillery shells at the Nikopol district of Dnipropetrovsk region last night. iy
