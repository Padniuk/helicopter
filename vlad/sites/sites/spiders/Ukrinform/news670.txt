On September 21, 2022, nine civilians were killed and 37 injured due to the Russian armed aggression within the territory of Ukraine.

The relevant statement was made by Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Kyrylo Tymoshenko on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. Most fatal casualties were recorded in the Donetsk region: five civilians killed and 12 injured. In addition, two civilians were reported killed and two injured in the Dnipropetrovsk region, one killed and 12 injured in the Kharkiv region, one killed and eight injured in the Zaporizhzhia region, three injured in the Kherson region. A reminder that a total of 391 children have been killed and over 767 injured in Ukraine since the Russian invasion started. mk
