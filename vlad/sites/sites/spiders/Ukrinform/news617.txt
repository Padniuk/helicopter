Head of the Ukrainian President’s Office Andriy Yermak had a phone conversation with U.S. President's National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan.

According to the Ukrainian president’s   the conversation took place as part of the ongoing dialogue with partners and allies. “Andriy Yermak informed the American side of the situation at the front, as well as the measures taken to ensure security in the de-occupied territories and return normal life there,” the report reads. The parties also discussed continuing security assistance for the Ukrainian army. Andriy Yermak thanked the American allies for standing side by side with the Ukrainian people in the fight against Russian aggression.
