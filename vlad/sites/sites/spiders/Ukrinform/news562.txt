The Russian Federation endangers the whole world and must be stopped before it is too late.

The relevant statement was made by Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky on  , commenting on Russia’s missile attack, which took place close to South Ukraine Nuclear Power Plant (NPP) in the Mykolaiv region last night, an Ukrinform correspondent reports. On the night of September 19, 2022, the Russian missile fell 300 meters away from South Ukraine NPP, having caused a short-term power outage. Windows were broken in the buildings situated within the area of South Ukraine NPP. “The invaders wanted to shoot again, but they forgot what a nuclear power plant is. Russia endangers the whole world. We have to stop it before it’s too late,” Zelensky stressed.
