Ten people were killed and 22 more were injured due to the armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine over the past day, September 18.

"Consequences of the armed aggression of the Russian Federation. Ten civilians were killed, 22 more were injured," Deputy Head of the President’s Office Kyrylo Tymoshenko posted on  . According to the regional military administrations’ data, two people were killed and two more were injured in Dnipropetrovsk region, three people were killed and eight more were injured in Donetsk region, one person was injured in Zaporizhzhia and Mykolaiv regions each, one person was killed in Sumy region, four people were killed and 10 more were injured in Kharkiv region. As reported, on September 18, Russian troops continued to attack the regions of Ukraine, civilians were killed and injured, infrastructure and residential buildings were destroyed. ol
