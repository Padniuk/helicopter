The Ukrainian military have shot down Russia’s Su-25 assault aircraft in the Kherson region.

The relevant statement was made by the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Around 08:00 a.m., September 19, 2022, the air defense missile unit of the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed a Su-25 assault aircraft of the Russian occupation troops,” the report states.
