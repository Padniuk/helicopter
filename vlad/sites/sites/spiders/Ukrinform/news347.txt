This morning the Ukrainian military have shot down Russia’s Ka-52 ‘Alligator’ attack helicopter in southern Ukraine.

The relevant statement was made by the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Around 08:00 a.m., October 7, 2022, the air defense missile unit of the Center Air Force Command of Ukraine’s Air Force shot down a Ka-52 ‘Alligator’ attack helicopter of Russian occupiers in southern Ukraine,” the report states.
