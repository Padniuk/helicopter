A Russian missile has hit a residential building in Ukraine’s Zaporizhia.

This was reported on Telegram by Acting Mayor Anatoliy  , according to Ukrinform. "As a result of an enemy attack on Zaporoizhia, a residential building was damaged. There are casualties," ​​the mayor wrote. The emergency response services are working at the scene of the strike. It should be recalled that on Friday, as a result of the shelling of Zaporizhia by Russian terrorists, the city's civil infrastructure was damaged and more casualties were reported.
