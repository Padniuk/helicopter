The Ukrainian Armed Forces have destroyed nine Shahed-136 kamikaze drones in the south of the country.

The Ukrainian Air Force said this in a post on  , Ukrinform reports. "On the night of October 5-6, Russian occupation forces attacked Ukraine with Iranian-made Shahed-136 kamikaze drones. Nine kamikaze drones were destroyed by the anti-aircraft defense forces of the Air Command South," the post read.
