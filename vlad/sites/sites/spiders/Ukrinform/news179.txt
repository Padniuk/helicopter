The enemy attacked Kryvyi Rih and Nikopol districts, Dnipropetrovsk region, with a kamikaze drone, Uragan, Grad MLRS, and heavy artillery.

"Nikopol district came under Uragan, Grad MLRS, and heavy artillery fire. In Nikopol town, private houses, outbuildings, and power lines were damaged," Valentyn Reznichenko, Head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration, posted on  . In Chervonohryhorivka community, the scale of destruction is being assessed. No casualties were reported. An enemy kamikaze drone hit a school in Kryvyi Rih town center at night. Two floors were destroyed, a fire broke out. No casualties were reported. The situation in other districts of the region was calm. ol
