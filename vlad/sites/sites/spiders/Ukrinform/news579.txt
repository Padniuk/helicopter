At a training area near Torez, Donetsk region, the Russian command has set up a training camp for convicts.

According to Ukrinform, the press service of the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine reported this on  . "At a training area near the city of Torez, the occupiers set up a training camp for the training of personnel who arrived from prisons to join the units of the 1st Army Corps that suffered losses during the hostilities," the report said. It added that the enemy continues to take radical measures to keep the situation under control in the Zaporizhzhia region. The command of the 58th Combined Arms Army of the Russian occupation forces held a meeting with the commanders of units operating in the region. According to the available information, participants in the event drew attention to the need to hold the occupied positions and prevent the breakthrough of the Ukrainian defense forces deep into the occupied territory. Meanwhile, in the temporarily occupied territory of the Luhansk region, collaborators and local residents of the Bilokurakyne and Troitske districts loyal to the occupiers, who actively cooperated with the occupation authorities, are moving to Russian territory. They are trying to take with them documents confirming their illegal activities as members of self-proclaimed authorities, the press service said.
