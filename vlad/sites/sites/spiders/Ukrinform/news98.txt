Four people have been killed and 18 injured in a Russian missile attack on the city of Dnipro and the Dnipropetrovsk region.

Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Kyrylo Tymoshenko said this in a post on  , Ukrinform reports. "As of now, four people have been confirmed dead, and 18 have been injured. My condolences to loved ones," he said. 
             
			 Tymoshenko published a photo of a crater that shows the impact of the Russian missiles, adding that the invaders "are hitting the civilians who lived around." According to him, the Russians also shelled the Dnipropetrovsk region with Uragan multiple rocket launchers, in particular the village of Kamianske in the Nikopol district. Tymoshenko said that another rocket hit the territory of a non-operating flour mill in the village of Kolomiitseve.
