Ukraine’s National Guard servicemen have used a drone to destroy an enemy ammunition depot hidden in the forest belt.

The relevant video was posted by the National Guard of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Early in the morning, our special forces destroyed a small ammunition depot placed by [Russian] orcs in the forest belt. The National Guard members watched the delivery and unloading of ammunition. After that, using a drone, they launched a fragmentation grenade there,” the report states.
