The Russian invaders have shelled the Nikopol and Novomoskovsk districts of Dnipropetrovsk region.

Mykola Lukashuk, the head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Council,   this on Telegram, according to Ukrinform. "In the Nikopol district, the occupiers struck the Chervonohryhorivka community with barrel artillery. No casualties were reported," he wrote. It is noted that a Russian missile struck the Novomoskovsk district. Experts are working at the site. There were no victims among the civilian population. In other districts of Dnipropetrovsk region, no emergency situations have been recorded. As Ukrinform earlier reported, the enemy shelled three communities of Dnipropetrovsk region.
