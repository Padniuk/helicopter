Russian troops last night shelled the Dnipropetrovsk region using Grad multiple launch rocket systems and heavy artillery.

Dmytro Reznichenko, the head of the regional military administration, said this on his  channel, Ukrinform reports. "The Russians fired nearly 70 deadly shells at the Nikopol district last night. They hit towns and villages in the Marhanets and Nikopol communities using Grads and heavy artillery. According to preliminary information, no people were injured," he wrote. According to him, several private houses, a parking lot, a pharmacy, several shops, a painting workshop and an agricultural enterprise were damaged in Nikopol. Three cars burned down and nine were damaged by shelling. A power line was broken. Several private homes, a solar power plant, industrial and food enterprises were damaged in the Marhanets community.
