Last night Russian troops launched a massive missile attack on the city of Zaporizhzhia. Civil infrastructure was damaged, and casualties among civilians were reported.

The relevant statement was made by Zaporizhzhia Acting Mayor Anatolii Kurtiev on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “In apartment blocks and detached houses, windows were damaged. Some houses have their roofs destroyed. Educational institutions and a hospital were also damaged,” Kurtiev wrote. Relevant services are working at the scene. Municipal workers are getting to work. Casualties among civilians were reported; information about them is yet to be checked. A reminder that enemy missile strikes were recorded in several districts of Zaporizhzhia and a village situated nearby in the Zaporizhzhia district. Three civilians were reported injured. A power substation was left without electricity in one of districts. mk
