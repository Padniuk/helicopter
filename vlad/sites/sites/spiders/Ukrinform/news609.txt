Russian invaders shelled the city of Sloviansk in the Donetsk region again this afternoon, with enemy shells hitting the area of a furniture workshop.

Vadym Liakh, head of the Sloviansk civil-military administration, said this on  , Ukrinform reports. "Unfortunately, the situation in Sloviansk is tense again. The territory of a furniture shop on Heolohichna Street came under fire in the afternoon. Probably, [the enemy used] S-300 [missiles]. Fortunately, there are no casualties," Liakh wrote. He also published a video and photos of the destruction from the site of the shelling.
