On the Crimean Bridge, which connects Russia to the temporarily occupied Ukrainian Crimea, fuel tanks are burning and a section of the road has been destroyed.

The Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine   this on Telegram, Ukrinform reports. "Morning on the Crimean Bridge. Fuel tanks have caught fire. A section of the road has been destroyed! Everything will be Ukraine!" the Air Force wrote.
