People got trapped in one of the houses of Kholodnohirsky district as the Russians launched a missile attack on Kharkiv city.

"Missile attack on Kholodnohirsky district. Apartment blocks were hit," Kharkiv Mayor Ihor Terekhov posted on  . He added that the information about the casualties was being clarified. According to Terekhov, people got trapped in one of the houses as a result of the missile strike, and rescuers are doing everything possible to save them.
