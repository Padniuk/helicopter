In the city of Zaporizhzhia, the bodies of two people were found under the rubble of a destroyed building hit by a Russian missile this morning.

According to an Ukrinform correspondent, the liquidation of the consequences of the missile attack continues in the city center. Rescuers are dismantling the rubble of a multi-storey building hit by an S-300 missile. The bodies of two people were found. Works are ongoing. About 100 rescuers, the police and the Red Cross are working at the scene of the tragedy.
