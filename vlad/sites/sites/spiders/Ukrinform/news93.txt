The Ukrainian military has shot down a Russian Orlan-10 drone in the Sumy region, using small arms.

Dmytro Zhyvytskyi, the head of the Sumy regional military administration, said this in a post on  , Ukrinform reports. "In the Sumy region, the Ukrainian military 'brought down' a Russian 'bird' forever. Today, in the area of responsibility of the Operational-Tactical Grouping Sumy, minus one Russian Orlan-10 that crossed the border of Ukraine," he wrote. On September 29, the enemy also shelled the Seredyna-Buda community in the Sumy region with self-propelled guns and mortars. No casualties and destruction were reported.
