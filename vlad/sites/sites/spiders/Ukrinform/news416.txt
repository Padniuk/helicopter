Russian invaders killed three civilians and injured 14 in the Donetsk region on October 8.

According to Ukrinform, Pavlo Kyrylenko, the head of the regional military administration, said this in a  post. "On October 8, the Russians killed three civilians in the Donetsk region: two in Chasiv Yar and one in Bakhmut. Another 14 people were injured," he wrote. According to him, it is currently impossible to establish the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha.
