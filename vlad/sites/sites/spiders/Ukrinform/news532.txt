Russian invaders are forcing people to build fortifications in the Kherson region.

Yurii Sobolevskyi, the first deputy head of the Kherson Regional Council, said this on the messaging app  , Ukrinform reports. "Reports have emerged again of the civilian population of the Kherson region being forced to do 'community work,' including during the construction of defensive fortifications," the report said. Sobolevskyi also added that everyone affected by these actions of the occupiers should inform Ukraine's SBU State Security Service. Anonymity is guaranteed.
