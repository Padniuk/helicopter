The Time magazine has dedicated the cover of its new issue and an article to the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, General Valeriy Zaluzhny.

According to Ukrinform, the magazine reported this on  . "TIME's new cover: Inside the Ukrainian counterstrike that turned the tide of the war," the tweet reads. Zaluzhny was earlier included in the list of the most influential people in 2022 according to the Time magazine. In April, the Time magazine dedicated the cover of its new issue and an article to the leadership of Ukraine's President Volodymyr Zelensky during the resistance to Russian aggression. In July, the magazine dedicated the cover of its new issue and an article to the leadership of the Ukraine's First Lady, Olena Zelenska.
