Five explosions have been heard in the village of Bohatyr, Melitopol district, where Russian invaders deployed personnel and military equipment.

The relevant statement was made by Melitopol Mayor Ivan Fedorov on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Right now, five explosions have taken place in the village of Bohatyr, near Molochnyi Estuary, where the occupiers redeployed some of their military equipment and personnel,” Fedorov wrote. Around 04:00 a.m., explosions were also heard in the village of Radyvonivka. The enemy’s losses are yet to be checked.
