On Thursday, September 22, the Russian military shelled 12 border communities of Sumy and Chernihiv regions.

"Yesterday, the enemy shelled 12 border communities of Sumy region and Chernihiv region with mortars and artillery," the State Border Guard Service of Ukraine posted on  . In Sumy region, four people were injured, 20 houses, two enterprises, a school, a kindergarten, and a cultural center were damaged. The night in the regions was calm.
