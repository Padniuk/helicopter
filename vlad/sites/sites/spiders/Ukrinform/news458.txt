The Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine has destroyed Russia’s Su-25 assault aircraft in the sky over the Kherson region.

The relevant statement was made by the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Around 08:30 a.m., September 16, 2022, in the sky over the Kherson region, the air defense missile unit of the Odesa Brigade of the South Air Command destroyed Russia’s another Su-25 assault aircraft,” the report states.
