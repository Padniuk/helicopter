On the night of September 29, 2022, Russian invaders struck the city of Mykolaiv, causing damage to a number of civilian objects.

The relevant statement was made by Mykolaiv Mayor Oleksandr Sienkevych on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Experts are examining the impact area. Therefore, I will report the details later. Now I can only note that civilian objects have been damaged,” Sienkevych wrote. Sienkevych also posted a photograph, showing the fragments of the Russian missile. A reminder that, on September 27, 2022, a total of 56 civilian and infrastructure objects were damaged in Russia’s shelling of Mykolaiv. mk
