Another victim who was injured in Russia’s missile attack on a convoy of civilian cars in the city of Zaporizhzhia, which took place on September 30, 2022, has died in hospital today.

The relevant statement was made by the Main Department of the National Police of Ukraine in the Zaporizhzhia region, an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “As a result of missile strikes on a civilian column at a car market, launched on September 30, a total of 32 people were killed and 91 injured as of today,” the police said. On   TV Channel, Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration Head Oleksandr Starukh noted that another victim had died in hospital. A reminder that, on September 30, 2022, Russian troops struck a convoy of civilian cars at a checkpoint in the city of Zaporizhzhia with S-300 missiles.
