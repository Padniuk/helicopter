On Thursday, September 22, Russian troops struck Slovyansk, Donetsk region.

"Yesterday evening the town came under fire again. S-300 [missiles were fired]. The area of the meat processing plant was hit. Private houses on Michurina Street were damaged," Vadym Liakh, Head of the Slovyansk Town Military-Civilian Administration, posted on  . No casualties were reported.
