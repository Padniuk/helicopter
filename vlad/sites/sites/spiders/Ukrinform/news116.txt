Russian troops launched a rocket attack on a humanitarian convoy heading to the temporarily occupied territories.

"The enemy launched a rocket attack on a civilian humanitarian convoy on the way out of the regional center. People were in line to leave for the temporarily occupied territory to take their relatives and deliver aid," Oleksandr Starukh, Head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration, posted on  . People were killed and injured. Rescuers, medics, and all relevant services are currently working at the site.
