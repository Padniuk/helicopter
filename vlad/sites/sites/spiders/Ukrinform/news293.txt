Rescuers have extinguished the fires caused by enemy suicide drones in the Kyiv region’s Bila Tserkva.

The relevant statement was made by the Ukrainian State Emergency Service on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Rescuers have extinguished the fires at the site of suicide drone attacks in the city of Bila Tserkva,” the report states. On October 5, 2022, Russian troops launched a series of suicide drone attacks on infrastructure facilities in the Kyiv region’s Bila Tserkva, resulting in huge fires. A total of 57 rescuers and 15 fire and rescue equipment units were working at the scene. A reminder that six Shahed-136 suicide drones attacked the Kyiv region’s Bila Tserkva on the night of October 5, 2022. mk
