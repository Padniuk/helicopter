Last night Russian troops struck the city of Kharkiv with the S-300 missiles launched from Russia’s Belgorod region.

The relevant statement was made by Kharkiv Regional Prosecutor’s Office on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. Two districts came under enemy fire in Kharkiv. In the Novobavarskyi district, the Russian missile hit a critical infrastructure facility, having caused a fire. A 46-year-old female watcher was killed. The other missile struck an enterprise in the Kholodnohirskyi district. Destructions were reported.
