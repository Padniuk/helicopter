Four civilians have been killed in Russia’s missile strike on the city of Pervomaiskyi, which is situated in the Kharkiv region.

The relevant statement was made by Spokesperson for Kharkiv Regional Military Administration Olena Shapoval, an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Four civilians were killed: a 15-year-old girl, two men and a woman,” Shapoval told. Rescuers continue to carry our dismantling works at the scene. A reminder that, on September 26, 2022, Russian troops launched a missile strike on a civil infrastructure facility in the Kharkiv region’s Pervomaiskyi. In addition, critical infrastructure was damaged, and two detached houses were destroyed. Water supply services were interrupted on some streets. mk
