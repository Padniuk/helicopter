The Russian army has attacked the city of Chuhuiv, Kharkiv region, with missiles, injuring a child.

Oleh Syniehubov, the head of the Kharkiv regional military administration, said this on the messaging app  , Ukrinform reports. "The occupiers launched missile strikes on Chuhuiv. Unfortunately, an 11-year-old girl was injured. She was hospitalized," Syniehubov wrote. He added that according to preliminary data, the strikes caused damage to critical infrastructure, private buildings, an enterprise, and a gas station.
