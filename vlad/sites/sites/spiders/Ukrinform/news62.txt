In the town of Mykolaivka, Donetsk region, rescuers recovered the body of a woman from the rubble of a school partially destroyed by Russian shelling.

"Upon arrival, it was established that the first to third floors of the three-story building of school No.1 were partially destroyed as a result of shelling (according to preliminary data, rocket attack). As a result of emergency and rescue operations, the body of a dead woman born in 1959 was removed from the rubble," the State Emergency Service of Ukraine posted on  . Rescuers removed 50 tonnes of structural elements of the building. As reported, Russian troops fired at a school in Mykolaivka, Donetsk region, where a shelter for civilian population had been set up, around 05:00 on September 28. ol
