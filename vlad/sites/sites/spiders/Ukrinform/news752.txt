The Russian forces launched five strikes on the Nikopol district, Dnipropetrovsk region.

Valentyn Reznichenko, the head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration,   in a Telegram post, Ukrinform reports. "The enemy fired more than 170 shells from Grad MLRS and barrel artillery at three communities of the Nikopol district - Chervonohryhorivka, Nikopol and Marhanets.
