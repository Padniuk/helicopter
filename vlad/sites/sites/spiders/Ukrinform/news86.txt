The number of the injured in Russian Uragan MLRS strike on Zelenodolsk community, Kryvyi Rih district of Dnipropetrovsk region has risen to 19.

"The number of the injured in the morning enemy attack on Zelenodolsk community has risen to 19. People were just going to work when the Russians fired their Uragan rockets at them," Valentyn Reznichenko, Head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration, posted on  .
