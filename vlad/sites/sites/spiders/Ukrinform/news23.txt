The Armed Forces of Ukraine shot down a Russian unmanned aerial vehicle on Tuesday, September 27.

According to Ukrinform, Ukraine's Air Command South reported this on  . "On September 27, at about 11:00, in the Kherson district, during an attempt to conduct aerial reconnaissance and adjust fire, air defense forces and means of the Air Command South eliminated a Russian operational-tactical drone, probably Orlan-10," the report said. Also on September 27, the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed three Iranian-made Shahed-136 drones that attacked Odesa.
