Russia's armed aggression against Ukraine killed 11 civilians and wounded 25 on Monday, September 26, according to regional military administrations.

Deputy Head of the Ukrainian Presidential Office Kyrylo Tymoshenko said this in a post on  , Ukrinform reports. According to the infographics published by him, eight civilians were killed and six injured in the Kharkiv region, three killed and 13 injured in the Donetsk region, and six injured in the Dnipropetrovsk region. Tymoshenko earlier said that five people had been hospitalized after a Russian missile attack on Kupiansk, Kharkiv region.
