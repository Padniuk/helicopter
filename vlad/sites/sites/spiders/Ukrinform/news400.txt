In the Kryvyi Rih district, a combine harvester was blown up after it ran over an enemy mine. The driver is alive.

Yevhen Sytnychenko, the head of the Kryvyi Rih District Military Administration,   this in a Telegram post, Ukrinform reports. "Near the settlement of Velyka Kostromka, the combine driver, while harvesting sunflowers, drove over an enemy mine, which exploded. The combine was damaged. But the main thing is that the driver is alive," said Sytnychenko. He reminded that mine clearance works are underway in the district. However, the process is quite long, he stressed. iy
