Ukraine’s Air Force units have destroyed five enemy operational-tactical level unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and one Ka-52 helicopter over the past day.

The relevant statement was made by the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “On September 17, 2022, across different directions, the air defense missile units of the Air Force destroyed five operational-tactical level UAVs and one Ka-52 helicopter,” the report states. Ukraine’s bombers, fighter and assault aircraft launched about 10 air strikes on Russian occupation troops. As a result, enemy personnel and military equipment clusters, field ammunition depots and air defense systems were struck.
