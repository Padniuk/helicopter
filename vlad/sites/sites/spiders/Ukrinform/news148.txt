Four civilians have been killed and six injured in Russia’s shelling of the Donetsk region over the past day.

The relevant statement was made by Donetsk Regional Military Administration Head Pavlo Kyrylenko on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “On September 30, 2022, Russians killed four civilians in the Donetsk region: two in Maksymilianivka, one in Bakhmut, and one in Sviatohirsk. Six more people were injured,” Kyrylenko wrote. At the moment, it is also impossible to estimate the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha, Kyrylenko added. A reminder that, on September 29, 2022, eight civilians were killed and 17 injured in Russia’s shelling of the Donetsk region. mk
