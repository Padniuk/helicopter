A powerful explosion has rocked the temporarily occupied city of Melitopol, where the pseudo-referendum is being held today.

Melitopol Mayor Ivan Fedorov said this in a Telegram  , Ukrinform reports. "Just now: a powerful explosion in the northern part of Melitopol. According to preliminary information, a car is on fire. We are collecting detailed information," he wrote. As earlier reported, explosions rocked the cities of Melitopol and Berdiansk in the first half of the day.
