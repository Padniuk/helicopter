The Russians struck Nikopol district, Dnipropetrovsk region, from Grad MLRS.

"At night, the Russians hit Nikopol district with Grad MLRS and heavy artillery three times. Nikopol town came under fire twice. Around midnight, almost 30 Russian shells slammed into the town.
