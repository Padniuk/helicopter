The Ukrainian anti-aircraft gunners have shot down Russia’s Mi-8 helicopter.

The relevant video was posted by the 53rd Separate Mechanized Brigade named after Prince Volodymyr Monomakh of the Ground Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “During the enemy air attack, thanks to our guardians of the sky, the [Russian] occupation army lost their Mi-8 helicopter. The anti-aircraft gunners launched a precise strike, and in just seconds the helicopter was burning down on the Ukrainian black soil,” the report states.
