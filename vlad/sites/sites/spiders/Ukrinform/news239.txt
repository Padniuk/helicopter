Russian troops launched a rocket attack on Kharkiv in the early hours of Tuesday, killing a woman.

Oleh Syniehubov, the head of the Kharkiv Regional Military Administration, said this in a statement on  , according to Ukrinform. "After midnight, one rocket hit an enterprise in the Kholodnohirsky district. A fire broke out. According to preliminary information, there are no casualties," he said. According to Syniehubov, the rocket attack damaged critical infrastructure in the Novobavarsky district. There was also a fire. "Unfortunately, a 46-year-old woman died," Syniehubov wrote. According to him, law enforcement officers are working at the sites of the attacks and recording the crimes of the Russians. On October 3, the Russian military shelled a medical facility in Kupiansk, Kharkiv region, killing a doctor and injuring a nurse.
