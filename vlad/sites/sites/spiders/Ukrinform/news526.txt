Yesterday, Russian invaders killed five civilians in Donetsk region.

"On September 17, the Russians killed five civilians in Donetsk region: two – in Bakhmut, two – in Vuhledar, and one – in Heorhiyivka. Eighteen more people were injured," Pavlo Kyrylenko, Head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration, posted on  . He noted that it was currently impossible to establish the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha. As reported, Donetsk region is constantly under enemy fire, there is no gas supply in the region, there are problems with water supply. The enemy shells and destroys educational institutions, healthcare facilities, cultural centers, houses, and all civilian infrastructure. Mandatory evacuation was announced in the region. ol
