On Friday, the Russian invaders directed three Shahed-136 kamikaze drones to Odesa. One of the drones was destroyed by the Ukrainian air defense forces.

The Odesa City Council   this on Telegram with reference to the Operational Command South. "Odesa was attacked by kamikaze drones from the sea. Two drones destroyed an administrative building in the port area, rescue services are fighting the fire," the report reads. One civilian was killed, according to the city council.
