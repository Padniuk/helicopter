The Russian forces fired ten S-300 missiles at Zaporizhzhia on Tuesday morning, damaging infrastructure facilities.

Oleksandr Starukh, the head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration,   in a Telegram post, Ukrinform reports. "The enemy again struck infrastructure facilities in the regional center with ten S-300 missiles. Fires broke out in the affected areas. Rescuers are working there," he wrote. As a result of the attack, a power line was damaged. Relevant services are restoring the network. According to preliminary information, there were no casualties.
