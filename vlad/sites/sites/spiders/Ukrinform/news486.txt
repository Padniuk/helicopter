The Russian forces used Grad MLRS and heavy artillery to shell the Nikopol district, Dnipropetrovsk region.

Valentyn Reznichenko, the head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration,   in a Telegram post, Ukrinform reports. “The Russians shelled the Nikopol district with Grad MLRS and heavy artillery all night... Three communities of the district - Nikopol, Marhanets and Myrove - came under fire. The enemy attacked six times - fired more than 90 deadly artillery shells at peaceful towns and villages," he wrote. <script async src="https://telegram.org/js/telegram-widget.js?19" data-telegram-post="dnipropetrovskaODA/1873" data-width="100%"></script>  According to preliminary information, no one was injured. Houses, cars, a lyceum, an industrial enterprise’s workshop, gas pipelines and power lines were damaged. In one of the villages of the Myrove community, the mobile network was not available. The scale of  destruction is being clarified. In other areas of the region, air raid sirens rang out at night, but there were no attacks. The situation is calm now. iy
