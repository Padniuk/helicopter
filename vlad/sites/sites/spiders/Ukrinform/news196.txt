The Ukrainian military destroyed two Russian Ka-52 helicopters and four drones over the past day.

The press service of the Joint Forces Task Force published a video showing the destruction of the helicopters on  , Ukrinform reports. "Air defense units in eastern Ukraine destroyed two Ka-52 helicopters, three Orlan-10 UAVs and a Shahed 136 suicide drone over the past 24 hours," the report said. Earlier reports said that in the Kharkiv region, Ukraine's National Guard soldiers captured an enemy ZALA drone.
