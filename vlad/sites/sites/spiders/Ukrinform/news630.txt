Mykhailo Podolyak, the advisor to the head of the Ukrainian President’s Office, has commented on Vladimir Putin declaring partial mobilization in Russia.

The official posted his comment on  , as seen by Ukrinform. “210th day of the ‘three-day war’. Russians who demanded the destruction of Ukraine ended up getting: 1. Mobilization. 2. Closed borders, blocking of bank accounts. 3. Prison for desertion. Everything is still according to the plan, right? Life has a great sense of humor,” the advisor tweeted on September 21. As Ukrinform reported earlier, Russian President Vladimir Putin signed a decree on partial mobilization, which is kicking off immediately.
