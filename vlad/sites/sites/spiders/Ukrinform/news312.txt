Andriy Yermak, the head of the Ukrainian President's Office, has commented on a rocket attack on apartment buildings in Zaporizhzhia, stressing that Russian terrorists are able to fight only with civilians.

He said this in a statement on  , Ukrinform reports. "Zaporizhzhia. Rocket attacks on apartment buildings. Russian terrorists are able to fight only with civilians. Crazy cowards," he wrote. Yermak said the "Russian issue" must be resolved "once and for all." "This can only be done by force. Weapons, sanctions, isolation. We continue to work. Total defeat of the enemy and prosecution. That's the only way," he said. In the early hours of October 6, the Russian army fired seven rockets at Zaporizhzhia, destroying two multi-story residential buildings. Two people are known to have died in the attack.
