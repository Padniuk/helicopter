Russian troops have launched a missile strike on the Donetsk region’s city of Kurakhove. Three civilians were reported injured.

The relevant statement was made by Donetsk Regional Military Administration Head Pavlo Kyrylenko on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Russians have launched a missile strike on Kurakhove this morning. As a result, three civilians were injured, six apartment blocks damaged. Explosives experts, rescuers and the police are working at the scene,” Kyrylenko wrote. Kyrylenko once again called on local residents to evacuate to safer areas, as the Donetsk region’s entire territory remains at high risk due to Russian attacks.
