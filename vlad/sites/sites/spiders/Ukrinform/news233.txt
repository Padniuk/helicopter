Ukrainian air defense units shot down a Russian missile over Kryvyi Rih district of Dnipropetrovsk region.

"Today, our air defense units shot down an enemy missile in Kryvyi Rih district. The consequences are currently being clarified. So far, no casualties have been reported," Mykola Lukashuk, Head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Council, posted on  . During the day, the occupiers shelled Nikopol with tube artillery. A fire broke out, which was already put out by rescuers. One ferry burned down. People were not injured. A 14-year-old girl was injured in a Russian missile attack on the village of Maryanske, Kryvyi Rih district, at night. According to updated information, Maryanske was hit with a Tochka-U missile. Grain storages, docks and special equipment of the agricultural enterprise, four private residential buildings, an outbuilding, and 300 solar panels were damaged. As reported, on the morning of October 3, the Russian army struck Dnipro district of Dnipropetrovsk region, and shelled Nikopol and Kryvyi Rih districts last night. ol
