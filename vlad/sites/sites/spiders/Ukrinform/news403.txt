In Sviatohirsk, Donetsk region, the bodies of 21 civilians killed during the Russian occupation of city have been reburied.

Pavlo Kyrylenko, the head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration,   in a Telegram post. "During the battles for Sviatohirsk and the occupation, the deceased people were buried in the streets and yards. All of the bodies reburied today have been identified and properly interred," he wrote. As reported, the exhumation and reburial of those who died during the Russian occupation began in Lyman and Sviatohirsk of Donetsk region. The Ukrainian army liberated Sviatohirsk on September 12. iy
