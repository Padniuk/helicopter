A total of 22 civilians have been killed and 32 injured in Russian attacks on the territory of Ukraine over the past day.

The relevant statement was made by Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Kyrylo Tymoshenko on  , referring to the data from military regional administrations, an Ukrinform correspondent reports. In particular, 12 civilians were killed and 12 injured in the Zaporizhzhia region, two injured in the Mykolaiv region, nine injured (including one in mine blast) in the Kharkiv region, one killed and two injured in the Kherson region. A reminder that, on October 5, 2022, a total of 20 civilians were killed and 22 injured in Russian attacks on the territory of Ukraine. mk
