The Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed a Russian helicopter and a Su-25 fighter aircraft.

According to Ukrinform, the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces   this on Telegram. "On September 25, around 17:00, units of the Odesa anti-aircraft missile brigade of the Air Command South shot down an enemy Su-25 fighter aircraft that had attacked our troops in Kherson region," the command said. To rescue the pilot, the invaders sent a Mi-8 helicopter with a team on board, which was also destroyed by the anti-aircraft gunners of the Odesa brigade.
