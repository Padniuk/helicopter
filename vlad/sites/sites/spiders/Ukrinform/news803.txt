President Volodymyr Zelensky has stated that Russia will not be able to subdue Ukraine with missiles, as with such attacks it only brings itself closer to international isolation and economic degradation.

He   this on Telegram and published photos showing the consequences of enemy missile attacks on the residential area of Kharkiv — Saltivka, Ukrinform reports. “Kharkiv. Saltivka. A large and peaceful residential area. It was like that until Russia came. But no missile will bring the terrorist state closer to its goal. The subjugation of Ukraine will not happen. Instead, each missile brings Russia closer to greater international isolation, greater economic degradation, and even clearer historical condemnation,” Zelensky wrote.
