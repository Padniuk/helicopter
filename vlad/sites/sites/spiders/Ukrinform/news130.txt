Russia's armed forces killed 36 civilians and injured 69 more on September 29.

Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Kyrylo Tymoshenko said this in a statement on  , Ukrinform reports. According to him, the Russians killed 23 and injured 28 civilians in the Zaporizhzhia region. Eight people were killed and 17 wounded in the Donetsk region. In addition, three civilians were killed and 11 wounded in the Mykolaiv region, one killed and five wounded in the Dnipropetrovsk region, one killed and one wounded in the Kherson region, and seven wounded in the Kharkiv region. Russian troops this morning fired three S-300 missiles at a humanitarian convoy near the Zaporizhzhia-Orikhiv highway. According to the Ukrainian Prosecutor General's Office, 25 people have been confirmed dead in the attack.
