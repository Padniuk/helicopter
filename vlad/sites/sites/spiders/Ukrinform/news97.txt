The Ukrainian military have shot down two Russian Su-25 assault aircraft in the Mykolaiv region’s Bashtanka district.

The relevant statement was made by the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Around 05:00 p.m., September 29, 2022, in the Mykolaiv region’s Bashtanka district, Odesa Anti-Aircraft Artillery Brigade’s servicemen shot down two Russian Su-25 assault aircraft,” the report states. In addition to four Kh-59 missiles, three enemy Orlan-10 unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV) were confirmed to have been destroyed over the past day, the Air Force Command noted. mk
