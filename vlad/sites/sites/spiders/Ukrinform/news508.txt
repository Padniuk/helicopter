This afternoon, Russian troops shelled two districts of Dnipropetrovsk region.

Mykola Lukashuk, the head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Council,  in a Telegram post, Ukrinform reports. "The enemy opened fire with barrel artillery on the Marhanets community. There, 10 private residential buildings, 6 farm buildings, two cars, a gas pipeline and a power line were destroyed. No one was injured," he wrote In the evening, the Pokrovske community of the Synelnykove district came under enemy fire. The extent of the destruction is being clarified. According to preliminary information, one man was injured. In other areas of Dnipropetrovsk region, the situation is calm.
