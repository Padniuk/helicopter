Artillery military units and divisions of the command of the Marine Corps of the Naval Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed two enemy mortars with crews and munitions, an Akatsiya self-propelled howitzer, and a warehouse with armored personnel vehicles of the invaders.

"On September 24, military units and divisions of the command of the Marine Corps of the Naval Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine continued repelling the full-scale armed aggression of the Russian Federation. Artillery military units and divisions of the command of the Marine Corps of the Naval Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine inflicted fire damage on the invaders, destroying two enemy mortars with crews and munitions, an Akatsiya self-propelled howitzer, and a warehouse with armored personnel vehicles," the press service of the Naval Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine posted on  . The enemy losses are being updated.
