Four apartments were completely destroyed and six more were partially destroyed in Russia's shelling of Mykolaiv last night. Rescuers saved all people trapped under the rubble.

Kyrylo Tymoshenko, the deputy head of the Ukrainian President’s Offices,   this on Telegram, Ukrinform reports. “Mykolaiv. An updated information. As a result of the shelling, four apartments were completely destroyed, six were partially destroyed. As of 8:00 a.m., works on removing the rubble of the damaged facilities were completed. All people were rescued! I’m grateful to the emergency services for their prompt work," he wrote. Tymoshenko also shared photos and videos of the consequences of the shelling. iy
