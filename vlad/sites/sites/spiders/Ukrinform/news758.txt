Residential houses have been slightly damaged in Russia’s suicide drone attack on the city of Odesa.

The relevant statement was made by Odesa Mayor Hennadii Trukhanov on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “As for the damage, according to the preliminary data, residential houses and buildings around the impact area were damaged slightly,” Trukhanov noted. In his words, public transport services will resume soon. A reminder that, in the morning of September 25, 2022, Russian troops attacked the city of Odesa with suicide drones. The enemy struck an administrative building situated in downtown Odesa three times. Rescue and firefighting operations were underway. One enemy drone was shot down by the Ukrainian anti-aircraft defense units. No casualties were reported. mk
