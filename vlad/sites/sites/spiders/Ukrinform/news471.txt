Over the past week, about 50 bodies of killed civilians were found in the liberated territories of Kharkiv region.

"About 50 people were found this week in the territory of Kharkiv region. We are not talking now about mass graves," National Police chief Ihor Klymenko said at a briefing, an Ukrinform correspondent reports. Regarding the found mass graves, Klymenko said that the police are currently checking the information. "We will be able to speak about the number more clearly later," he added.
