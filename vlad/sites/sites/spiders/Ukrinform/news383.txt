From February 24 to October 8, the Armed Forces of Ukraine eliminated about 62,060 Russian invaders, including 380 soldiers over the past day.

This is stated in the report of the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine posted on  . In addition, the Ukrainian troops destroyed 2,472 (+6 over the past day) Russian tanks, 5,111 (+18) armored personnel vehicles, 1,459 (+4) artillery systems, 345 (+1) MLRS, 180 (+3) anti-aircraft systems, 266 aircraft, 234 (+1) helicopters, 1079 (+12) UAVs of operational-tactical level, 246 cruise missiles, 15 ships / boats, 3,875 (+13) vehicles and fuel tanks, 136 (+1) special equipment units. The enemy suffered the biggest losses in Donetsk, Bakhmut, and Kramatorsk directions. The data are being updated, the General Staff noted. ol
