Soldiers from the 128th Mountain Assault Brigade raised the national flag of Ukraine at the highest point of the village of Myroliubivka, Kherson region.

Zakarpattia Legion said this in a Facebook  , Ukrinform reports. "Fighters of the 128th Separate Mountain Assault Zakarpattia Brigade raised the State Flag of Ukraine at the highest point of Myroliubivka, Kherson region," the report reads. The village of Myroliubivka was liberated by soldiers of Zakarpattia Legion. Earlier, President Volodymyr Zelensky officially confirmed that the urban-type settlement of Arkhanhelske and the village of Myroliubivka, Kherson region, were liberated from the Russian occupiers.
