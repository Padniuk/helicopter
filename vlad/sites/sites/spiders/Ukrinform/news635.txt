Russian forces have launched a missile strike on the city of Zaporizhzhia.

According to Ukrinform, Oleksandr Starukh, the head of the Zaporizhzhia regional military administration, reported this on  . "According to preliminary reports, the missiles hit infrastructure facilities. The information is being clarified," the report said. Before that, Starukh wrote on Telegram about the consequences of an enemy attack on the village of Hryhorivka, Zaporizhzhia district. In particular, the enemy strike caused considerable destruction to a local school. There are no casualties.
