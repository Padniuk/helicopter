Ukrainian marines hit a Russian BMP-2 infantry fighting vehicle and destroyed an ammunition depot on September 29.

The Ukrainian Navy said this in a  , Ukrinform reports. In addition, artillery military units of the Command of the Ukrainian Naval Infantry Corps destroyed three Giatsint-S self-propelled guns, an infantry fighting vehicle and heavy vehicles. The final losses of the enemy are being clarified. Between February 24 and September 30, Ukrainian defenders killed about 59,080 Russian soldiers, including 500 on September 29. In the past day, Ukrainian forces also destroyed two aircraft and a helicopter.
