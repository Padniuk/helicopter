Russian invaders shelled the Esman, Shalyhyne and Seredyna-Buda communities of the Sumy region on September 24.

Dmytro Zhyvytskyi, the head of the Sumy regional military administration, reported this on  , according to Ukrinform. "Esman community: the Russians launched five mines today. Shalyhyne community: almost at 14:00, there was an MLRS shelling attack - six explosions. Seredyna-Buda community: from 15:30, the enemy launched an artillery shelling of the town of Seredyna-Buda - five explosions," Zhyvytskyi said. He also added that no casualties or destruction had been recorded.
