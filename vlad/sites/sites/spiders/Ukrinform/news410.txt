Russian invaders have attacked the city of Zaporizhzhia with ten missiles, with the consequences of the attack being investigated.

Oleksandr Starukh, the head of the Zaporizhzhia regional military administration, said this in a  post. "The enemy continues to terrorize the city of Zaporizhzhia. Almost a dozen new missile strikes. The consequences are being established," Starukh wrote. At the same time, Anatolii Kurtiev, acting mayor of Zaporizhzhia, wrote on  that residential buildings had been damaged in the rocket attack. "Zaporizhzhia is again under massive rocket fire. Residential buildings were damaged in the enemy attack. A fire broke out," Kurtiev wrote. Information about casualties is being clarified. The relevant services are already working on the scene. Photos and videos from the scene were published on social media.
