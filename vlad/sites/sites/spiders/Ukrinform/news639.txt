On September 21, 2022, five Russian missiles struck three districts in the city of Zaporizhzhia.

The relevant statement was made by Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration Head Oleksandr Starukh on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Infrastructure facilities and detached houses were damaged. Two projectiles hit a field on the outskirts of the city,” Starukh wrote. Three people were reported injured. One of them was taken to hospital with concussion; his condition is stable. mk
