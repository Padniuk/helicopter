The enemy hit apartment blocks in Zaporizhzhia city. Casualties have been reported.

"The enemy fired missiles at residential buildings in Zaporizhzhia, destroying them significantly. Casualties have been reported," Oleksandr Starukh, Head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration, posted on  . The rescuers are already removing people from the rubble. Later, Starukh specified that the enemy had fired seven missiles at Zaporizhzhia. The missiles hit apartment blocks, entire entrances have been destroyed. People are trapped under the rubble. "Terror and killing of civilians in Zaporizhzhia! Racists fired seven missiles at apartment blocks. The number of casualties is being determined, people are under the rubble," he wrote.
