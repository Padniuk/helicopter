Russian troops have launched a missile attack on Zaporizhzhia and its outskirts.

Oleksandr Starukh, the head of the Zaporizhzhia regional military administration, said this in a statement on  , Ukrinform reports. "The enemy launched another rocket attack on the regional center and its outskirts. Stay in shelters. There is a risk of repeated shelling," Starukh wrote. Three explosions were heard in some districts of the city. This morning, the invaders shelled Zaporizhzhia with missiles. One of them fell in the yard of a high-rise building. One person was wounded by the shelling.
