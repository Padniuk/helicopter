Several explosions rocked Zaporizhzhia last night. According to preliminary information, the enemy hit two infrastructure facilities.

Oleksandr Starukh, the head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration,   in a Telegram post, Ukrinform reports. "The enemy launched another missile attack on the regional center. So far, there have been eight attacks. According to preliminary information, infrastructure facilities were once again the target," the report reads. Information about the victims is being clarified. In some areas of the city, electricity was cut off after the explosions. However, the power supply was restored within half an hour.
