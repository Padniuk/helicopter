Over the past day, the troops of the Russian Federation killed three residents of Donetsk region and injured eleven more.

"On September 25, the Russians killed three civilians in Donetsk region: two in Avdiivka and one in Bakhmut. Another 11 people were injured," Pavlo Kyrylenko, Head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration, posted on  . It is currently impossible to establish the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha, Kyrylenko noted. As reported, Russian troops struck Kramatorsk on September 25.
