The Russian invaders struck Zelenodolsk community in Dnipropetrovsk region with heavy artillery, injuring a 58-year-old woman.

"The Russians struck Zelenodolsk community with heavy artillery. The destruction scale is being specified. A 58-year-old woman was injured. She was just riding a bicycle when the Russian shells slammed into the road," Valentyn Reznichenko, Head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration, posted on  . According to Reznichenko, the woman is now in the hospital. Doctors assess her condition as moderate.
