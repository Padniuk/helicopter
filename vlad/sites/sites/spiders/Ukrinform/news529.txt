The Armed Forces of Ukraine killed about 54,480 Russian soldiers between February 24 and September 18, including 230 in the past day alone.

The General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine said this in a  post, according to Ukrinform. Also, Ukraine's defenders destroyed 2,210 (+8) main battle tanks, 4,718 (+17) armored fighting vehicles, 1,309 (+3) artillery systems, 312 (+0) multiple rocket launchers, 168 (+0) air defense systems, 251 (+1) warplanes, 217 (+1) helicopters, 918 (+7) operational and tactical-level unmanned aerial vehicles, 238 (+2) cruise missiles, 15 (+0) warships/cutters, 3,578 (+7) trucks and tankers, and 122 (+1) pieces of specialized equipment. The figures on enemy losses are being verified, the General Staff said.
