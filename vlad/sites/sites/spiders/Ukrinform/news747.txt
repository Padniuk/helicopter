Infrastructure facilities were destroyed and three people were injured in Zaporizhzhia as a result of enemy missile strikes.

Oleksandr Starukh, the head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration,  this in a Telegram post, Ukrinform reports. "Enemy missile strikes were recorded in several districts of Zaporizhzhia city and a village near the regional center in the Zaporizhzhia district. Information about the victims is being clarified. As of now, we know about three wounded people," he said. Starukh specified that infrastructure facilities were destroyed. In one of the districts, a power substation was cut off.
