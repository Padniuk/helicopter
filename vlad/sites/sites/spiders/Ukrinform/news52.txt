Russian invaders have dropped phosphorus bombs on a village in the Zaporizhzhia region.

The Zaporizhia Separate Territorial Defense Brigade announced this in a post on  , Ukrinform reports. "These munitions set fire to everything that burns. Civilians suffer the most from them. Several houses and a school burned down," the post read. In the last 24 hours, in the Zaporizhzhia region, Russian invaders used tanks, mortars and field artillery near Mykilske, Prechystivka, Neskuchne, Novomykolaivka, Vremivka, Temyrivka, and Olhivske.
