In Bila Tserkva, Kyiv region, rescuers put out a fire caused by enemy kamikaze drones this night. Three infrastructure buildings were damaged.

Oleksiy Kuleba, Head of the Kyiv Regional Military Administration, announced this during the nationwide telethon, Ukrinform reports with reference to Kuleba's Telegram  . According to him, "all necessary safety measures were taken and the fire caused by six kamikaze drone strikes was put out. Three infrastructure buildings were damaged." "The life of an injured person is not in danger," the head of the Administration noted. As reported, the Russian invaders attacked Bila Tserkva town, Kyiv region, with Shahed-136 kamikaze drones on October 5. ol
