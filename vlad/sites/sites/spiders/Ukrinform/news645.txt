A 13-year-old boy and four adults were injured in a Russian strike on Kupyansk, Kharkiv region.

"Today, five people, including a 13-year-old boy, were injured in the enemy shelling of Kupyansk. Other victims are men aged 36, 47, and 63, and an 83-year-old woman," Oleh Synehubov, Head of the Kharkiv Regional Military Administration, posted on  . In addition, a 55-year-old woman was injured in a Russian strike on the village of Strilecha, Kharkiv district. The injured were hospitalized and receive medical aid. ol
