One civilian has been killed in Russia’s shelling of the Donetsk region over the past day. In addition, four civilian bodies were found during exhumation procedures in the liberated city of Sviatohirsk.

The relevant statement was made by Donetsk Regional Military Administration Head Pavlo Kyrylenko on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “On October 4, 2022, Russians killed one civilian in the Donetsk region, at Maiorsk Station in Zaitseve. Six more people were injured,” Kyrylenko wrote. In Sviatohirsk, during exhumation procedures, four civilians were found killed. At the moment, it is also impossible to estimate the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha, Kyrylenko added.
