Russian troops shelled the city of Kramatorsk in the Donetsk region on Saturday, injuring at least three people.

According to Ukrinform, Kramatorsk Mayor Oleksandr Honcharenko said this in a  post. "An enemy attack on Kramatorsk. Private buildings. Three people are known to have been wounded. Rescue operations are ongoing," he said. On September 16, Russian troops struck the Donetsk region 13 times, shelling seven settlements. Civilian casualties were reported.
