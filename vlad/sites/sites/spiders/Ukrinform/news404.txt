The Armed Forces of Ukraine shot down another Russian unmanned aerial vehicle.

"On October 8, around 19:30, on the approach from the sea to Odesa region, forces and means of air defense of the Air Command South eliminated another Russian drone of operational-tactical-level," the Operational Command South   on Facebook.
