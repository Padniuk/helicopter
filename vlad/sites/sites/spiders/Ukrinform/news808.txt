Russian troops have again opened fire on the Donetsk region’s Sloviansk. Information about casualties and the damage caused is yet to be checked.

The relevant statement was made by Sloviansk City Military Civil Administration Head Vadym Liakh on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “In Sloviansk, it is restless again. The enemy shelling near Barvinkivska Street and in the central part, Botanichna Street and Shevchenka Street. Information about casualties and destructions is yet to be checked,” Liakh wrote. Liakh also posted the photographs, showing the aftermath of Russian attacks: a deep crater left by the enemy projectile; the fragments of the unexploded shell; the roof broken in the residential house. A reminder that, on September 26, 2022, four civilians were injured in Russia’s shelling of Kramatorsk. mk
