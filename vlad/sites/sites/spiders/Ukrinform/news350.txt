One of Russian missiles has hit the yard of an apartment block in the city of Zaporizhzhia, causing damage to the residential house.

The relevant statement was made by Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration Head Oleksandr Starukh on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “One of enemy missiles hit the yard of an apartment block. The residential house suffered destructions; public utility systems were damaged,” Starukh wrote. One civilian was reported to have received shrapnel injuries. Medical aid is now being provided to him. Information about casualties is yet to be updated. Relevant services are working at the scene.
