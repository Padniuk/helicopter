From February 24 to September 20, the Armed Forces of Ukraine eliminated about 54,810 Russian soldiers, including 160 servicemen over the past day.

This is stated in the report of the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine posted on  . Moreover, the defenders of Ukraine destroyed 2,216 (+2 over the past day) Russian tanks, 4,724 (+4) armored personnel vehicles, 1,323 (+10) artillery systems, 318 (+6) MLRS, 168 anti-aircraft systems, 252 (+1) aircraft, 217 helicopters, 925 (+5) operational-tactical UAVs, 239 (+1) cruise missiles, 15 ships/boats, 3,587 (+6) vehicles and fuel tanks, 125 (+3) special equipment units.
