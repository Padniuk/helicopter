Five civilians were hospitalized following a Russian missile strike on Kupiansk, Kharkiv region.

According to Ukrinform, Kyrylo Tymoshenko, the deputy head of the Ukrainian Presidents Office,   this on Telegram and shared a relevant video. "The video shows where the occupiers are targeting and the consequences of these attacks. A church school, a farm building and a shop were on fire. No military facilities," he said. According to Tymoshenko, five people were hospitalized due to enemy shelling. All of them are residents of the Kupiansk district. He stressed that the Russians "ran away.
