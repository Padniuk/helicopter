The Ukrainian defense forces have shot down a Shahed-136 kamikaze drone, which Russian troops used in an attempted attack on Mykolaiv.

According to Ukrinform, the public liaisons service of Ukraine's Air Command South reported this on  . "On September 25, at about 15:00, the air defense forces of the Air Command South shot down a Shahed-136 kamikaze drone, which the Russian occupation forces used in an attempted attack on Mykolaiv," the report said. On September 25, the Russians again attacked Odesa with kamikaze drones. The enemy hit an administrative building in the center of the city three times.
