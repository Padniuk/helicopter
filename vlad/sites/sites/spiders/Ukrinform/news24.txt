From now on, two modern robots for remote demining of areas will help bomb disposal experts of the main office of the State Emergency Service of Ukraine in the Vinnytsia region.

The press service of the State Emergency Service said this in a post on  , Ukrinform reports. "The cost of the equipment is almost four-and-a-half million hryvnias. This became possible thanks to the cooperation of rescuers with the Vinnytsia branch of the United Ukrainian American Relief Committee, Inc. The equipment will be sent to the territories liberated from the enemy to help our bomb disposal experts," the State Emergency Service said. The rescuers thanked Ukrainian friends and partners for their support.
