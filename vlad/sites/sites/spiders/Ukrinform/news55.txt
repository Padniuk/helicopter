A total of 6,326 Ukrainian children have been found since the Russian invasion started.

The relevant statement was made by the Ombudsman’s Office in Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Following the data from the Children of War state portal, as of September 28, 2022, a total of 241 children were listed as missing, 7,833 – deported, 6,326 – found,” the report states.
