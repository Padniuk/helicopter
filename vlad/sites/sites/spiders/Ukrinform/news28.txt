Over the past day, the Ukrainian marine units have destroyed two Russian tanks, one Msta-S self-propelled howitzer, two armored fighting vehicles and two ammunition depots.

The relevant statement was made by the Naval Forces Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “The artillery military units and the marine units of the Naval Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine inflicted fire damage on [Russian] invaders. As a result, our military destroyed two tanks, one Msta-S self-propelled howitzer, two armored fighting vehicles and two ammunition depots. The enemy’s final losses are yet to be checked,” the report states.
