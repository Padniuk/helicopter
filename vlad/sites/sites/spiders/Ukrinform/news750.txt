Two civilians were killed and eight more were injured in Russian shelling of Donetsk region on September 24.

Pavlo Kyrylenko, the head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration,   in a Facebook  post, Ukrinform reports. "On September 24, Russians killed two civilians in Donetsk region – in Bakhmut. Eight more people were injured,” he wrote. Overall, 884 civilians have been killed and 2,233 injured in Donetsk region since the full-scale invasion started. The regional governor noted that it is currently impossible to determine the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha. iy
