Two Shahed-136 drones and one Mohajer-6 UAV were shot down in Ukraine’s Mykolaiv region on Thursday.

That’s according to Ukraine’s  , Ukrinform reports. "On October 6, between 09:00 and 11:30, on the approach from the sea to Mykolaiv region’s littoral, forces and means of air defense of Air Command South eliminated two enemy kamikaze drones Shahed-136 and one attack drone Mohajer-6," the statement reads. As Ukrinform reported earlier, the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed nine Shahed-136 kamikaze drones in the country’s south.
