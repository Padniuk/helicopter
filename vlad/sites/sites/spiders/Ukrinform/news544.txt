The Russian troops shelled Nikopol district of Dnipropetrovsk region with Grad MLRS. Two people were killed, three more were injured.

"Two men were killed. Two more men and a woman were injured... Such are disappointing preliminary data after the mass shelling of Nikopol district..." Valentyn Reznichenko, Head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration, posted on  . It is noted that the Russians struck three communities of Nikopol district four times in the afternoon. More than 60 shells were fired from Grad MLRS and heavy artillery. There is destruction in the communities. The scale is being specified. Rescuers and police work at the scene. ol
