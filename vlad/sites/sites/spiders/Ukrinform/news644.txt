In Sumy region, the Russian troops shelled the border village of Volfyno, Bilopillia community, with mortars. Three houses were damaged and one burned down.

"The Russians shelled the border village of Volfyno, Bilopillia community. The enemy fired 10 shells that slammed into the houses of civilians. As a result, three houses were damaged by bursts. One house burned down," Dmytro Zhyvytskyy, Head of the Sumy Regional Military Administration, posted on  . According to preliminary data, local residents were not injured. The scale of the destruction is being clarified. As reported, Russian troops again shelled Chervonohryhorivka and Marhanets communities of Nikopol district, Dnipropetrovsk region.
