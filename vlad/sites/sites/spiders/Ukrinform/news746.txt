A powerful explosion rang out on Saturday in the northern part of Melitopol, temporarily captured by the Russians.

Melitopol Mayor Ivan Fedorov said this on  , Ukrinform reports. "Resistance forces once again do not allow the occupiers to sleep peacefully. Just now: a loud explosion in the northern part of Melitopol," he wrote. The mayor said that according to eyewitnesses, a fire engine had gone in that direction. "We are gathering information on where the occupiers had trouble this time," Fedorov added. Earlier reports said that a loud explosion rocked the center of temporarily occupied Berdiansk on Saturday.
