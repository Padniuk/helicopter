Five civilians have been injured in Russia’s shelling of the Donetsk region’s Kostiantynivka today, including a child.

The video showing the aftermath of Russian attacks on Kostiantynivka was posted by Donetsk Regional Military Administration on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. One can see the apartment blocks damaged and left without windows, doors, lots of broken glass, construction debris, enemy projectile fragments. “Kostiantynivka is located farther away from the front line, but Russians are continuously striking the city,” the regional authorities noted.
