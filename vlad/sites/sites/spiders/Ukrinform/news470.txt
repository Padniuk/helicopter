Two men were killed and another one was injured in the enemy shelling of Dnipropetrovsk region.

"Russians killed two men, 48 and 52, in Velyka Kostromka. Another man was injured. He is in the hospital. Doctors assess his condition as moderate," Valentyn Reznichenko, Head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration, posted on  . It is noted that Russian troops struck Zelenodolsk community with heavy artillery in the afternoon. The center of Velyka Kostromka was hit. There is destruction in the village.
