In the Mykolaiv region, soldiers of the Kherson anti-aircraft missile brigade shot down a Shahed-136 suicide drone using a large-caliber machine gun.

The Command of the Ukrainian Air Force said this in a  post, Ukrinform reports. "On the morning of October 8, in the Mykolaiv region, soldiers of a mobile fire group of the Kherson anti-aircraft missile brigade shot down a Shahed-136 kamikaze drone with an Utyos large-caliber machine gun," the report said. The command stressed that Ukrainian anti-aircraft gunners will not stop until "they turn the Russian invaders in the Ukrainian sky into scrap metal." Late on October 7, Ukraine's air defense forces shot down three Iranian-made Shahed-136 kamikaze drones used by the Russian invaders to attack the Odesa and Mykolaiv regions.
