A total of 6,289 children have been listed as found and 7,894 deported since the Russian invasion started.

The relevant statement was made by the Ombudsman’s Office in Ukraine on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Following the data from the Children of War state portal, as of October 4, 2022, a total of 239 children were listed as missing, 7,894 – deported, 6,289 – found,” the report states.
