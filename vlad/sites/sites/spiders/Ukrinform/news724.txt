The Armed Forces of Ukraine eliminated about 56,300 Russian soldiers between February 24 and September 24, including 240 in the past day alone.

The General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine said this in a  post, Ukrinform reports. Also, Ukraine's defenders destroyed 2,262 (+8) enemy tanks, 4,807 (+11) armored fighting vehicles, 1,361 (+6) artillery systems, 327 (+1) multiple launch rocket systems, 170 (+0) air defense systems, 255 (+1) warplanes, 220 (+1) helicopters, 956 (+6) operational-tactical UAVs, 240 (+0) cruise missiles, 15 (+0) warships / cutters, 3,681 (+22) trucks and tankers, and 126 (+0) pieces of specialized equipment. Over the past day, the enemy suffered the greatest losses in the Kramatorsk and Donetsk directions, the report said.
