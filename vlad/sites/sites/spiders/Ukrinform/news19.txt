The Ukrainian defense forces have destroyed three Shahed-136 kamikaze drones today, September 27.

According to Ukrinform, the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine  this in a Telegram post. "On September 27, around 8:00, the air defense forces of the Naval Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed all three Shahed-136 kamikaze drones, which Russian troops used to attack Odesa," the report reads. It is noted that the Ukrainian defense forces are looking for effective methods of countering barrage munitions and gaining more experience in destroying kamikaze drones.
