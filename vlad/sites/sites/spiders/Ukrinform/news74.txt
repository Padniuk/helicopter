The residential areas in the city of Dnipro have been struck with a Russian missile. Three civilians, including a child, were reported killed and five injured.

The relevant statement was made by Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration Head Valenyn Reznichenko on  , an Ukrinform correspondent reports. “Russians struck Dnipro with missiles at night, having hit residential areas. At the moment, three civilians have been reported killed, including a child… Five were injured, including a 12-year-old girl,” Reznichenko wrote. Several detached houses were destroyed completely. Rescuers continue dismantling the rubble. More victims may be remaining trapped there. Over 60 detached houses and several apartment blocks were damaged in Dnipro. Enemy projectiles also caused damage to a local market, buses, cars and power transmission lines. mk
